Privacy Policy

GymTrainLog — Last updated: April 2026

GymTrainLog is a free Android application for personal trainers to manage their trainees, workout plans, and session logs.

Data collection

GymTrainLog does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter in the app (trainee names, exercises, session logs) is stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database. Nothing is sent to any server.

Internet access

The app contains a single link to its GitHub repository, which opens in your device's browser. No data is sent as part of this action — it is a standard browser link.

Third-party services

The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Permissions

The app requests the following Android permission:

  • VIBRATE — used by the rest timer to alert you when a rest period ends. No data is accessed or transmitted.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If the privacy practices of the app change, this page will be updated. The date at the top reflects when it was last revised.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on the GitHub repository.
